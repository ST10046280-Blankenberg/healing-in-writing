> Healing-In-Writing is a non-profit based in Cape Town that helps trauma survivors reclaim their stories through journaling workshops, community events, and practical support. This app is a simple website to share stories, list events, guide visitors to help, and support volunteers and admins.

---

## Purpose of the site
<div style="border-left:4px solid #FFA92C; padding:0.75rem 1rem; background:#FDF5ED;">
<ul>
  <li>Give survivors a safe platform to share approved stories</li>
  <li>Show events and allow simple RSVPs</li>
  <li>Share donation details clearly (bank info and QR only, no online payments here)</li>
  <li>Help people find support resources (hotlines, centres, legal steps)</li>
  <li>Help volunteers log hours and help admins approve and report on hours</li>
</ul>
</div>

## Who it serves
<div style="border:1px solid #E9D5EB; border-radius:12px; padding:1rem 1.25rem; background:#F6FAF9; box-shadow:0 6px 18px rgba(47,22,72,0.10);">
  <p style="font-style:italic; color:#2F1648;">Each audience receives targeted journeys while sharing the same compassionate tone.</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Guests: read stories, see events, view books on sale, find help, view donation details</li>
    <li>Volunteers: submit stories, RSVP to events, log hours, view their activity</li>
    <li>Admins: review and publish stories, manage events and books, approve hours, view reports</li>
  </ul>
</div>

## Content and tone
<div style="border:1px solid #E9D5EB; border-radius:12px; padding:1rem 1.25rem; background:#FDF5ED;">
  <p style="font-weight:600; color:#2F1648;">Guard survivor dignity and clarity at every touchpoint.</p>
  <ul style="color:#4B5563;">
    <li>Language must be clear, respectful, and survivor-centred</li>
    <li>Remove identifying details from stories unless explicit consent exists</li>
    <li>Use plain language. Avoid technical jargon</li>
  </ul>
</div>

## Privacy and safeguarding
<div style="border:1px dashed #2F1648; border-radius:12px; padding:1rem 1.25rem; background:#F6FAF9;">
<ul style="color:#4B5563;">
  <li>Follow POPIA principles</li>
  <li>Role-based access for volunteer and admin areas</li>
  <li>No secrets committed to the repo</li>
  <li>Error messages must not reveal sensitive information</li>
</ul>
  <p style="margin:0.75rem 0 0; font-size:0.95rem; color:#0C0C20;">Keep the trust of participants by challenging any feature that risks confidentiality.</p>
</div>
